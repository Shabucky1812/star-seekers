Star Seekers

Come and seek the stars with us...

Modern life is structured around daylight. We work in the daytime and sleep at night. Here at Star Seekers, we think that should change. As beautiful as the world can be when it is lit up by the sun, we think the true beauty of our planet is unveiled under the stars. We want you to see that beauty too, that is what Star Seekers is all about.

Our team of incredible guides host night-time events at various times and locations with all sorts of different themes for you to choose from. Our events are usually an hour in length and involve: meeting your fellow seekers for the night, taking a short tour of the night's location, and finally stopping and truly embracing the sky at the event's star-stop!

To get started, check out the Events page to see a list of upcoming events. If any take your fancy, all you have to do is click on the event to view more details about it. From there you can ask a question and view questions asked by others. Most importantly, after creating an account you can book your place at any event you like. We can't wait to see you soon!
